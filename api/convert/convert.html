Unit Conversions¶

Here we list functionality converting between units and unit sets.

Converting units of the same dimensions¶

This section shows API related to converting units of similar sets. For example, m/s and miles/hr both have dimensions of length/time.

convert_temp

Converts temperature from one unit set to another

convert_unit

Converts units between two unit sets

debye_to_einstein

Converts Debye temperature to Einstein temperature

einstein_to_debye

Converts Einstein temperature to Debye temperature

Converting units of different dimensions¶

These are conversions between unit sets. For example, finding the vibrational temperature (dimensions of temperature) to vibrational frequencies (dimensions of inverse time).

energy_to_freq

Converts energy to frequency

energy_to_temp

Converts energy to temperature

energy_to_wavenumber

Converts energy to wavenumber

freq_to_energy

Converts frequency to energy

freq_to_temp

Converts frequency to temperature

freq_to_wavenumber

Converts frequency to wavenumber

inertia_to_temp

Converts moment of inertia into rotational temperature

temp_to_energy

Converts temperature to energy

temp_to_freq

Converts temperature to frequency

temp_to_wavenumber

Converts vibrational/rotational temperature to wavenumber

wavenumber_to_energy

Converts wavenumbers to energies

wavenumber_to_freq

Converts wavenumber to frequency

wavenumber_to_inertia

Converts wavenumber (1/cm) to moment of inertia

wavenumber_to_temp

Converts wavenumbers (1/cm) to temperatures (K)
