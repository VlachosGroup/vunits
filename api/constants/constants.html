Molar (univeral or ideal) gas constant.

Rydberg constant.

T0

Standard temperature.

Standard volume.

Speed of light.

Charge of electron.

Vacuum permittivity.

h

Planck’s constant.

kb

Boltzmann constant.

Mass of a electron.

Mass of a proton.

mu_0

Vacuum permeability.

r_bohr

Bohr radius.
